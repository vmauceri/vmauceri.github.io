Home Page

Welcome to Spend Less, Sea More, your ultimate guide to quality experiences on a simple budget. We believe that being a "global citizen" shouldn't be a title reserved for the wealthy. Whether you're looking to navigate though Southeast Asia's night markets or find hidden-gem hostels in Europe, we provide blue prints to help you go farther for less.

The Art of Travelling: Travelling is more than just a change of scenery: its a powerful way to experience personal growth while seeing the world through your own eyes. It forces us out of our comfort zones, broadening our perspectives while fostering immersion of various cultures. Whether it be navigating through a foreign market or solo hiking a silent trail, travelling sharpens your ability to adapt while giving you stories that last a lifetime.

What it means to "Travel on a Budget": Budget travelling isn't about deprivation, rather, its about prioritizing experiences over luxury. It requires the individual to be international with their resources - choosing street food over a fancy restaurant, or local transport over taxis - so you get the local experience.It is the art of finding value in the authentic rather than the expensive.

Budget Travel Across Different Lifestyles:

Saving while travelling completely varies on whos holding the map:

Solo: For a single person, budgeting usually involves hostel dorms, slower travel via buses, and a flexible schedule that allows plan changes within a few minutes to catch a deal.

Couple: Couple travelling can look like splitting costs by booking an airbnb with two hostel beds, cooking date night dinners instead of eating out every night and shopping from local markets.

Family: For larger groups, budgeting is a bit of a logistical puzzle. It looks like Air Bnbs with kitchens, seeking “kids-stay-free” deals, and focusing on free public spaces like beaches, museums, and parks that keep the little ones entertained while utilizing family discounts.
