Travel the Middle East

Do not believe what the media tells you! Traveling to the middle east does not have to be dangerous as the media portrays it. That does not mean you go to Iraq or Afghanistan to travel, but many other middle eastern countries have amazing things to see and explore. Saudi Arabia is a good place to start and not so expensive compared to Dubai or Abu Dhabi which are part of the UAE. Saudi has more of a cozy feel than the UAE in my opinion. People are hard workers and they like to enjoy themselves in the evening hours when it gets cooler.
