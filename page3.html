Travel Europe

Everyone assumes European travel is expensive! What they don’t understand is that it can be; it is up to the person or the family on how much they want to spend. It doesn’t mean it is unrealistic to go there on a budget as well. There are options out there for any place in the world but Europe can be very diverse on what you want to do, explore, see and how much you want to spend doing it, while still getting the full experience. Some European countries are super affordable such as Montenegro. North Macedonia uses their own currency and the exchange rates are amazing as well as Turkey, Albania, Romania and many nearing countries. You have to check and see where you want to travel and what currencies they use. Even the countries that use Euros can be affordable with a little research.
