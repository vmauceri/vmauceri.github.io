Travel Asia

Depending where you start your travel from, Asia is a great place for vacation for solo travelers and for families. They are super safe especially when traveling with small children and very affordable for cool things to do once you get there. As far as lodging, in Asia it is not necessary to stay at the same hotels you know back home. Their own little hotels are just as great- if not better- since you would be supporting the local economy and really getting the cultural experience. Also, do not shy away from good rated hostels. They do have family rooms available as well as single rooms if sharing is not your cup of tea. If you want to learn history and the ancient world, Cambodia is a great place to go, especially Angorwatt. Get a local Tuk-Tuk to take you around for about $20 a day if not cheaper. If you want amazing food Thailand is the place to go and their beautiful beaches. If you want to experience Communism a little on the safe side go to Vietnam and see the history and especially the war from their point of view. If you want to experience a little of muslim culture you will be blown away by Malaysia and what it has to offer.
