Cruising through Hawaii's Islands on a Real-Life Budget

Everyone's dream is to go to Hawaii. And they want to see everything and experience everything the islands have to offer. The best way and more economical way to do that is by taking a cruise. You know exactly how much it will cost you if you are planning to save up for the vacation and with a little research of your own you will find that renting a car and exploring yourself is much better especially with a family than booking with the cruise lines for the expensive excursions that they offer. You do not need a fancy SUV for island exploration of the islands, a small car will do and it can be as cheap as $50 a day if you shop around. They try to sell you extra things as it is and saving a few hundred dollars is a must if you are looking to save money. For example: the cruise line will try to offer you a WIFI package on the ship. It is absolutely unnecessary for that trip since Hawaii is America and you have a plan. Your phone will work on land and since you will only be sleeping on board you can go without internet connection for a few hours and really enjoy your time on the cruise. Most places in Hawaii were free to explore and at a minimal cost once you have your rental car and explore. You can find nice dining options on the budget and still enjoy the local food they have to offer. Google reviews come handy for the restaurants and things to see. You will spend the most amazing 7 days hopping from Island to Island on a cruise line, enjoying the entertainment on board as well as the all inclusive dinner and breakfast option which already is included in your price at booking the cruise. Some sites that are free are the coffee plantations, beaches with Sea turtles and so much more if you spend just a bit of time researching on your own.
